My travels

I did a lot of volunteering. I find it sad that it is overlooked by a lot of people just because it is not paid.

Even though, you are getting paid. Just not with money. For example, I have been a volunteer in ranches in Canada. I might not have received a paycheck, but I have been able to live the "real cow-boy life" as you see it on the movie, and it was amazing !

I have also been a volunteer for the Rugby World Cup and the Guinness 6 Nations France-Ireland. Not only good memories on both event (these loses hurt hard), but I have been able to live amazing things. Can you imagine that I was part of the crew that installed the fireworks for the entry of the players !
